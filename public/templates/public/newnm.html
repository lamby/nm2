Debian New Member - Join the NM process

If you are a DD, or have a guest account on Debian machines

In this case you have an entry in the site database: look it up, and send a link to your advocate(s). They can then:

  1. login;
  2. click on "advocate for DD" on the top right of the page;
  3. follow the simple instructions.

If you don't yet have an entry in the system

In this case you need to ask for a FD member to enter your information in the site database so that people can advocate you.

Please mail nm@debian.org taking care to:

  • Say that you want to become a Debian Developer.
  • Say if you also need to upload packages.
  • Cc your advocate(s).
  • Say what username you would like (minimum 3 characters, check db.debian.org to see if it is free, see DAManager for detailed account name policy).
  • Add anything else you may think is useful.
  • Sign the email with the key you use to sign your Debian work.

Here is an example you can use as a template. Don't forget to sign it with the right key:

From: $NAME <$EMAIL>
To: nm@debian.org
Cc: $ADVOCATE1@debian.org, $ADVOCATE2@debian.org

Dear Front Desk,

I would like join the NM process. $ADVOCATE1 and $ADVOCATE2 have agreed to
advocate my application.

My information is:

               First name: _________
              Middle name: _________ (if applicable)
                Last name: _________ (if applicable)
            Email address: _________
       Preferred username: _________ (check here for duplicates)
  OpenPGP key fingerprint: _________

I have been doing this and that with this and that person for so much time and
I could use DD privileges so that I could also do these and those other things.

--
$NAME
